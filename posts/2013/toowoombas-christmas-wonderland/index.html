Toowoomba's Christmas Wonderland

2013-12-16

Last night we went to check out the Christmas lights at Queen's Park. It was pretty much the same as last year, but I took some photos this time around.

We went to check out Audrey Ct as well, which we were told had some good lights. We were not expecting them to be as good as they were, almost every house in the street had put up a crazy amount of lights. I wouldn't want to live there this time of year with the number of cars driving past at 9:00pm on Sunday night when we went. Very impressive.
