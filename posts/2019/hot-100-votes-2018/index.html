Hottest 100 Votes for 2018

2019-01-20

  1. Holy Holy - Faces

    Such an awesome riff.

  2. Ocean Alley - Confidence

    This is the warm tunas pick for #1 spot, and well deserved. Also considered voting for Knees or Baby Come Back (which was easily the best Like A Version for the year).

  3. Childish Gambino - This is America

    Rachael is dissapointed I've voting for this, but it's a great song and and even better video clip.

  4. Didirri - Bird Sounds

    Didirri was the support for Vance Joy when we saw him earlier this year - but we didn't really know the songs and weren't overly impressed. The exception being this song and I Can't Get Last Night Out of My Head which have got better with each listen.

    We also heard Sometimes by Cub Sport that night for the first time and didn't like it - and yet it's grown heaps on me also.

  5. Vance Joy - Saturday Sun

  6. Skeggs - Up In The Clouds

  7. Ziggy Albers - Laps Around The Sun

  8. Ball Park Music - The Prefect Life Does Not Exist

  9. The Rubens - Never Ever (ft. Sarah)

  10. Methyl Ethel - Scream Whole

Heaps more on my shortlist this year, and was very difficult to narrow down.
